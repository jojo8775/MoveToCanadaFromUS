Directions to apply for Canadian PR through Express entry from USA.

Main steps of application :

  • Create a Profile and wait to receive ITA (Invitation to apply). To create your profile and get score, in-order to entry the pool following documents are needed.
    • WES evaluated degree, if you have a Canadian degree then your transcripts should be fine.
    • IELTS General test score.
  • If your profile gets picked in the monthly lottery based on your score, you will get an ITA. To complete your application you will have 60 days to upload the following documents
    • Police verification documents from USA and your home country
    • Medical exam results from the predefined list of practitioners.
    • Proof for funding.
    • non-refundable application fee
  • Once your documents are verified you will get a request to submit your passport and photo so that your COPR (also know as Confirmation of Permanent Residency) can be issued. The requested photo has specific requirements, so don't forget to read the instructions in the email. After receiving the COPR you will have 6 months to land to Canada via land or air to finalize your PR application.

The main overall documents which will be needed at various steps of your application:

  • Mark-sheet converted in Canada equivalent degree. This is needed only if you don't have a degree from Canada. If you have an advanced degree such as MS or Phd, only the most recent degree conversion is sufficient, and you may ignore the bachelors degree conversion.
    One of the organization which does foreign degree conversion is WES [link: https://www.wes.org/ca/eca/]
  • Police certification from your home country and from USA, if your home country is different from USA and you have been in USA for less than 10 years. US police certification can be obtained on-line from FBI website, and for the home country apply to the nearest Consulate.
  • Funding proof is needed based on you application profile. Usually last 6 months bank statement or 401k statement can be used to show as proof for funds.
  • Medical exam needs to be performed from a qualified medical officer, the list of practitioner can be found in the Canada govt website.
  • English language proficiency needs to be demonstrated by taking IELTS exam. (The General version)
